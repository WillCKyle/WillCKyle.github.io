Odomo

Odomo is an early-stage project building a consumer app leveraging web3 values and technology. Its core value proposition is connecting and transforming real-world communities.

Problem Discovery

The core problem that Odomo seeks to address is that it is extremely difficult for a broad swatch of civil society to coordinate to achieve a collective interest. Sometimes one organization will take initiative and others will end up following their lead, but this is often slow, uncoordinated and full of friction.

I spoke with activists trying to clarify the demands of a movement, DJs trying to establish music scenes, entrepreneurs trying to partner with other small businesses, and leaders in tech struggling to decide how to regulate their emerging industries.

The common thread I found between all of these experiences was that people were missing a way to establish productive consensus among diverse players with diverse interests. I began a long period of research and iterative development on a collective consensus-building model, which ultimately formed the nucleus of Odomo.
